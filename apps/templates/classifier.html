How Good Are Your Terms?

Terms of Services are hard to read, input a paragraph and get back a classification!

The colors below going from blue to red indicate the probability of an individual term being bad by using an ensembled Logistic Regression and Naive Bayes model. The data was pulled from tosdr.org, a website that allows the community to rate and judge individual terms of service.

Once you add a term (or multiple!) and analyze, you will be able to compare your terms to columns 1-5 that have been pre-populated with the terms of service from the User Content Section of Twitch, Tiktok, Youtube, Soundcloud, and Reddit.

This model should not be used for legal advice, consider the red dots as flags for further review.
